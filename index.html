The Myth of the Magic Millstones

In ancient times, there were mystical millstones that could grind not just grain, but also the very essence of the sea. Legend says that when these millstones were used improperly, they turned the seas salty forever.

According to the myth, the gods created two magical millstones that could grind anything. When misused, they unleashed an irreversible curse—turning all the waters of the earth into salty seas. The sea's saltiness was a mark of the gods' power and a reminder of the consequences of misuse.

The Magic Millstones

These millstones were believed to be crafted from rare stones that could harness immense power. It was said that whoever wielded them could control the forces of nature itself. But with great power came great responsibility.

The Saltwater Legend

The sea’s saltiness has been a subject of many ancient tales. The myth of the magic millstones is one of the most famous. It tells the story of a time before the oceans were filled with salt, when water was sweet and pure.
